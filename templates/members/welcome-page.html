Dear Member,

Thank you for considering joining the Wecan Youth Sacco Family! We are excited about the potential you will bring to our institution in the coming years.

Wecan Youth Sacco Limited, was formed and registered in the year 2013 by a group of friends who had a common value and purpose - to empower the youth of this country to save for a brighter future. Over the years, the team of friends has grown to become a family of young individuals who share a passion for financial growth & development, youth empowerment & community development. Our main focus in these years has been to encourage our members to save and invest at an early age in order to secure their future.

To this end, we have attached the documents on the right for your review and action should you wish to pursue your membership further. The documents include:

  1. Wecan Youth Sacco By-Laws
  2. Wecan Youth Sacco Membership Form
  3. Wecan Youth Sacco Nomination Form
  4. Wecan Youth Sacco Bank Details
  5. Wecan Youth Sacco By-laws Atestation
​

Kindly return the filled membership form as well as the nomination form to any of the officials copied herein.​

Kindly note that our registration fees is KES 500 and Share capital KES 1,000 while minimum savings is KES 500 per month.

Please feel free to reach out to us should you have any questions or require additional information.

We look forward to fruitful interactions in the future.
Thank you.
